What's anime?

Anime (アニメ):
Refers to hand-drawn and computer-generated animation originating from Japan.

Outside Japan and in English, anime refers specifically to animation produced in Japan. However, in Japan and Japanese, anime (a term derived from a shortening the word animation) describes all animated works, regardless of style or origin.

Anime's source material

Some anime have their source that they referred to. Some of them are:

Manga

Means comic in Japanese and the most common source material of anime.

Novels

This include webnovels, lightnovels and visual novels. Some even got adapted into manga before adapted into anime

Manhwa

Unlike manga, manhwa is korean comic. Usually come in the form of webcomic. Some of them got adapted by Japanese anime studio.
